AllBio metagenomics interoperability workshop (April 10-12, 2014, Amsterdam)

See the workshop page:

Supporting 'interoperability-lite' in the ELIXIR/BioMedBridges Tools & Data Services Registry

"The emerging ELIXIR/BioMedBridges Tools & Data Services Registry (http://bioregistry.cbs.dtu.dk/) helps the researcher find, understand, compare and select bioinformatics resources for their everyday work. As well as these discovery functions, the registry aims to inform a hacker of which tools might in principle be made to interoperate, and give them a head-start in implementing their workflows; so called 'interoperability-lite'. This seminar will showcase the registry and the underlying EDAM controlled vocabulary for the description of tools and data resources in terms of their function, data, formats and topics, and discuss requirements for interoperability, in general and specifically for metagenomics analysis."
