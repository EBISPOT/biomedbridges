About

This registry includes Web services and other software required by bioinformatics and biomedical researchers, especially those working within one of the ten biomedical sciences research infrastructures of BioMedBridges.

Please bear in mind that this version (beta version 03) - including the content - is a prototype. Ideas developed here, and recent content developed by BioMedBridges are carried forward into the ELIXIR Tools and Data Services Registry.

For more information mail Jon Ison cc'ing Kristoffer Rapacki.

The registry is developed by the Danish ELIXIR node in cooperation with BioMedBridges-funded partners.
