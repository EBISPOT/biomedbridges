About

The Tools and Data Services Registry will catalogue Web services and other software required by bioinformatics and biomedical researchers, especially those working within one of the ten biomedical sciences research infrastructures of BioMedBridges.

The registry is co-developed by BioMedBridges-funded partners and the Danish ELIXIR node.

This is the first beta release of the Query Interface. Please bear in mind that the content proper will be extensively improved once the Quality Assurance framework is developed and applied.

Beta version 01

There are no substantial functional changes since alpha_9. New content has been provided by CBS-DTU:

  • 60 new tools for sequence analysis, mostly analysis and prediction of protein functional sites and protein properties
  • 547 new annotations in total

See our plans for the next release.
